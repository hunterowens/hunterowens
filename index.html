About Me

Hi, I'm Hunter. You probably already knew that though. Anyways, I'm a student at the University of Chicago who spends less time on school than he should. I like working on projects that help communities use technology to make their lives better. Seriously. I've gotten to work with some pretty cool people, and for that, I'm grateful. I spend a lot of time thinking about urbanism, data pipelines and tacos. Let's talk.

Selected Projects

Bikeshare Predicton Project

Open source project to predict bikeshare station availability. Built for Divvy Bikes & Alta Bikeshare. Built scrapers, API, and managed historical database of bikeshare data.

Census Communities USA

A project with the US Census Bureau to provide an API for a variety of longitudinal employment datasets that the census hosts. Where we Work, a demo application, has been released.

Maroon Plaza

Designed and developed online clearinghouse and calendar for UChicago student events. (w/ Shuwen Qian) Built with Torando and MongoDB on an EC2 instance. Commissioned by the UChicago Student Leadership Project. [No Longer Active]. Source

Collegemat.ch

(With KIPP NJ) Did data-driven overview of the college applications process for KIPP NJ students, and then implemented suggestions into webapp for students and teachers.

Truckate

Truckate (Chicago Booth New Venture Challenge Finalist) was a webapp that utilized onboard devices to allow online ordering and mapping for food trucks.

LA News Desk

Co-Founder of independent Newspaper for high school students in LA area. [No longer active] (w/ Ethan Resnick)
